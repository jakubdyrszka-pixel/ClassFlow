## Users
- **Uczestnicy (Users)**: Fitness/yoga practitioners who need to view class schedules, book classes or join waitlists, track attendance, and show QR entry passes on their mobile devices.
- **Instruktorzy (Instructors)**: Teachers and trainers on-the-go who need to manage their schedule, run bulk check-ins, write session notes, and handle class cancellations.
- **Recepcjoniści (Receptionists)**: Frontend staff operating in high-tempo environments who need instant client search, manual payment recording, waitlist management, and rapid QR code scanning.
- **Administratorzy (Admins)**: Studio owners/managers who require deep analytics (revenue, class popularity), complete CRUD control over locations/rooms/instructors, recurring schedule rules (RRule), and logs.

## Product Purpose
ClassFlow exists to provide a seamless, all-in-one SaaS experience for fitness studios, yoga schools, and sports clubs. It aims to eliminate administrative friction for instructors and receptionists while ensuring a delightful, reliable booking experience for attendees. Success is defined by high system responsiveness, zero booking race conditions, and rapid front-desk check-ins.

## Brand Personality
- **Modern & Energetic**: Inspiring activity and health through clean typography and purposeful, vibrant accents.
- **Motivating**: Professional yet uplifting, encouraging users to engage with schedules and attend classes.
- **Clean & Focused**: High-contrast interfaces that prioritize clarity and readability, avoiding visual noise.

## Anti-references
- **Generic AI SaaS Slop**: Avoid standard purple-to-blue gradients, generic Inter-only fonts with tight display tracking, nested cards, and low-contrast grey text on colored panels.
- **Overly Playful/Childish UI**: Avoid clunky/oversized round borders, distracting animations, neon overload, or excessive emojis that compromise professional credibility.
- **Dry/Clinical Spreadsheets**: Avoid uninspiring, flat, default-styled tables or forms that make scheduling feel like a tedious chore.

## Design Principles
1. **Frictionless Velocity**: Action flows (especially receptionist check-ins and customer lookups) must be instant, requiring minimal clicks and cognitive load.
2. **Uplifting Rhythm**: Use deliberate spacing, vertical rhythm, and active accents to bring energetic movement to the UI without cluttering the screen.
3. **Contextual Isolation**: Dashboards must be strictly optimized for the specific role logged in, shielding users from unnecessary interface controls.
4. **Resilient Integrity**: Schedules, calendar slots, and booking statuses must use consistent, highly visible indicators so users never doubt the state of their classes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High Contrast**: Ensure body text and status tags hit WCAG AA (≥4.5:1) contrast ratios against backgrounds.
- **Responsive Adaptability**: Layouts must be fluid and fully usable on mobile screens for users and instructors checking schedules on-the-go.
- **Motion Control**: Respect user preferences by disabling animations for users with `prefers-reduced-motion`.
